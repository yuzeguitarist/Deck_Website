About Deck

Building the clipboard manager I always wanted. Simple, powerful, and respectful of your privacy.

My Story

Deck was born from frustration with existing clipboard managers. They were either too complex, too slow, or didn't respect user privacy. I knew there had to be a better way.

I set out to build a clipboard manager that feels like a natural extension of macOS. One that's fast enough to never slow you down, smart enough to find anything you've copied, and secure enough that you never have to worry about your data.

Today, Deck is used by users who demand the best from their tools. And I'm just getting started.

My Values

Privacy First

Your data belongs to you. Everything stays on your device, encrypted and secure.

Thoughtful Design

Every interaction is carefully considered. We believe great software should feel invisible.

Open Source

Transparency builds trust. Our code is open for anyone to inspect, contribute, or learn from.

Native Performance

Built with Swift for macOS. No Electron, no compromises, just pure native speed.

Open Source

Deck is proudly open source. I believe in transparency and community-driven development. Check out the code, report issues, or contribute to make Deck even better.

About Deck

Built for anyone whose work lives in the clipboard — native speed, clear workflow, privacy by default.

My Story

Deck started from a simple frustration: most clipboard tools felt bloated, sluggish, or careless with privacy.

The goal was clear: build a native macOS clipboard manager that feels instant, stays local, and scales from quick paste to advanced workflows.

Today, Deck continues to evolve around real usage — queue mode, LAN sharing, automation, and security-first defaults.

My Values

Privacy First

Your clipboard belongs to you. Data stays local, and sensitive access can be protected with Touch ID and encryption.

Thoughtful Design

Fast to open, easy to scan, and keyboard-first when you want speed.

Partial Open Source

Core modules are published for audit and contribution under GPL-3.0 with Commons Clause.

Native Performance

Built with Swift and system APIs for low latency and stable macOS behavior.

Partial Open Source

Deck is partially open source under GPL-3.0 with Commons Clause. Published modules are open for audit and contribution, while some components remain closed.

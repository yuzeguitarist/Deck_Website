Deck AI FAQ

Does Deck AI require one specific model provider?

No. Deck is built around a multi-provider model so you can switch between supported cloud providers or local Ollama models based on your workflow.

Can Deck AI modify data without asking?

Sensitive actions such as write, delete, or plugin modifications are approval-based. Deck is designed to keep users in control of high-impact operations.

Is this page the same as the AI crawl hub?

No. The crawl hub is for machine-readable discovery. This page explains the user-facing Deck AI product feature for people comparing AI clipboard tools on macOS.

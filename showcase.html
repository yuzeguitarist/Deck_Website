Introducing Deck Script Plugin

Describe the tool. Deck turns it into a working plugin.

Deck Script Plugin lets you describe the script you want in plain language, then turns that idea into a real plugin for your workflow. No JavaScript knowledge required anymore.

Use it to access the web, automate messy multi-step tasks, and build highly specific helpers in seconds. In this demo, AI writes a plugin and immediately uses it to process text with remarkable speed and judgment.

Meet Deck AI Memory

Memory, shaped by every conversation.

Deck AI Memory quietly captures the details you share in conversation and turns them into a living digital profile. The more you chat, the better it understands your habits, preferences, and context.

Everything is encrypted and stored locally on your device. Only the AI can read it, so your memories stay private, secure, and close to home.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Deck different from a typical macOS clipboard manager?

Deck prioritizes local-first privacy, semantic recall, queue paste workflows, and encrypted LAN sharing — not just a history list.

Does Deck upload my clipboard data?

Not by default. Clipboard history and search data stay entirely on your Mac.

Who should use Deck?

Developers, designers, researchers, and anyone who copies and pastes large amounts of content daily.
